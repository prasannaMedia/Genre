Our Concept &Data sets

GTZAN dataset is taken and loaded. The dataset consists of 1000 audio tracks, each 30 seconds long. It contains ten genres: blues, classical, country, disco, hip-hop, jazz, reggae, rock, metal, and pop. Each genre consists of 100 sound clips. Preprocessing the Data :- Before training the classification model, we have to transform raw data from audio samples into more meaningful representations. Feature Extraction :- The first step for music genre classification project would be to extract features and components from the audio files. It includes identifying the linguistic content and discarding noise.
